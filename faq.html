Frequently Asked Questions

This is an in-depth description about a few of the different classes, or fields of expertise you will get to experience in the upcoming game, Alibe, Libb Like Me.

What is Alibe: Libb Like Me?
- Alibe: Libb Like Me is a world-life RPG for Pc/Mac/Linux, Xbox One, Plyastation 4 & 5, Playstation VITA, Nintendo Switch, iOS and Android!
Who is making it?
- Alibe Libb Like Me 1.0 is being developed completely solo by Mortopolis (Daniel Dickerson), and it's initial start date was September 11th, 2022.
Who will the publisher be?
- Alibe: Libb Like Me is self-published by Mortapolis on all platforms.
Will Alibe: Libb Like Me have controller support?
- Yes! Alibe: Libb Like Me will support Xbox One and Xbox 360 controllers on PC, and has full controller support on Xbox One, Playstation 4 and 5 and Nintendo Switch.
What languages will the game support upon release?
- Upon release, Alibe: Libb Like Me will support German, Swedish, Spanish, French, Simplified Chinese, Japanese, and English.
What language is Alibe: Libb Like Me coded in?
- Python.
