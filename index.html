Alibe: Libb Like Me

Where dreams meet reality...

On a Planet formed from combining the Lands of Physical, Spiritual, Space, Time, Light, and Sound, to become the 6 realms of consciousness, you decide the fate of the Planet Alibe... In this mystic revolving world, we will portray a fluid and intangible sense of ideas to manifest a sense of upliftment and encouragement within each player's identity of themselves on 6 different continents and many locations inbetween to discover. We do this with the gameplay, music, artwork and overall feel of the game as you progress through the storyline and find the ultimate sanctuary in a liberated world!

Boundless Inspiration

Above is an example of a top down pixel role playing game from an early video game councel. This game played a massive portion in the development of the video game Alibe, and how the story progresses with an in-depth and impersonable experience. Below is a newer game depicted, which gave path to my relaxing and mindful setting of the game. Having a strengthened awareness of each detail within the making of the game enables the developer to fill stories around characters, objects, events, and others to a deeper level and a fuller meaning. These games give great leadway to what Alibe: Libb Like Me will hold!


Features

  • A fluid and intangible sense of ideas to manifest a sense of upliftment and encouragement within each player through a world of magic and adventure gameplay.
  • Explore 6 different realms and mysterious caves within Alibe, magic at hand.
  • Create the ultimate sanctuary in a liberated world using a variety of tools to restore the land with different vegetation, including ingredients for Alchemy and cooking!
  • Find the right way to express yourself in Alibe with over 500 customization options for your character!
  • Learn about living in Alibe with the mastery of 11 different classes to choose from, including the Sigil of the Knights, Gardners, Geocachers, Snorkelers and more!
