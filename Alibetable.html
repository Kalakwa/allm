Alibe Character Class Table

This is an in-depth description about a few of the different classes, or fields of expertise you will get to experience in the upcoming game, Alibe, Libb Like Me.

Class Title Class Description Class Description
Fisher Fisher is associated with successfully catching sea life within the game, through snorkeling, fishing and hook catching, mystic fish catching, or farming of fish. The fisher gains levels by accumulating experience points in this field. Miner Miner is asscoiated with successfully destroying minerals, stones, or locating precious gems and hard materials. The Miner skill is increased by breaking stones and other hard materials found within Alibe, and crafting items under the crafting catagory. Geocacher Geocacher is associated with the attention directed towards hidden clues within the terrain of Alibe. Throughout the game you will find 1, 2, 3, or a 4 hidden within the terrain of the game where you may gain experience points upon the completion of locating the cache. The number seen within the terrain indicates the level of treasure and difficulty to find the hidden cache may be. By noticing patterns specific to the location on terms of the ecosystem, societal trends, and land you are located, you will finally find a Blue peg, Green peg, Gold peg, or Platinum peg, respecive to the level of difficulty the geocache was listed.
